Services

Concrete Cutting

Our concrete cutting services specialize in four key areas: wall sawing, slab sawing, coring, and hand sawing. With our expertise and equipment, we are capable of handling a wide range of concrete cutting projects, from small-scale renovations to large-scale construction endeavors.

Concrete Coring

Our concrete coring services provide precise and efficient solutions for creating openings in concrete floors and walls. Whether you need to accommodate plumbing, drainage, HVAC systems, electrical wiring, ventilation, handrails, or fencing, we have the expertise and equipment to deliver exceptional results.
Our advanced coring techniques allow us to drill holes of various sizes and depths, ensuring that your specific needs are met. Our skilled technicians utilize coring equipment, including diamond-tipped drills, to minimize disruption and maximize accuracy.
We understand the importance of precision and safety in concrete coring. Our team adheres to strict safety protocols and industry best practices to ensure that our work is carried out efficiently and without compromising structural integrity.

Concrete Slab Cutting

Our specialized team offers expert warehouse floor cutting services tailored to meet your unique needs. We are proficient in creating trenches of any size for plumbing re-routing or other infrastructure modifications.
We have extensive experience in cutting both ground-level and suspended slabs. For suspended slabs, we employ safe and efficient lifting techniques to ensure the safe operation of our cutting equipment. Our team will also develop a comprehensive plan to safely remove the cut concrete debris.
With our advanced cutting equipment and skilled technicians, we are committed to delivering precise and efficient results, minimizing disruption to your warehouse operations.

Concrete Wall Cutting

Our hydraulic wall sawing services offer a precise and efficient solution for creating openings in walls for doors, windows, or other structural modifications. Our advanced hydraulic wall sawing equipment is capable of cutting through concrete walls up to 12 inches thick, ensuring minimal disruption to surrounding structures.
Our experienced technicians utilize hydraulic saws to deliver clean and accurate cuts, minimizing the need for additional finishing work. We prioritize safety and efficiency in all our operations, adhering to strict industry standards and best practices.

House Foundation Cutting

Our specialized team offers precise and efficient window and door cutout services, ensuring minimal disruption to your home. We have extensive experience in foundation cutting and take meticulous precautions to protect your interior spaces during the cutting process.
Our skilled technicians utilize advanced cutting equipment to create clean and accurate openings for your new windows or doors. We are committed to minimizing noise and dust pollution, ensuring a comfortable experience for you and your household.
Once the cutting is complete, we can also offer optional concrete removal services to streamline the project and simplify the cleanup process. Our team is equipped to safely and efficiently transport the removed concrete debris.

Green/Control Cutting

Timely expansion joint cutting is crucial for preventing uncontrolled cracking in green concrete. By cutting the joints within 1 to 2 days of pouring, you can effectively manage the contraction and expansion of the concrete, ensuring its structural integrity and longevity.
Our expert team specializes in expansion joint cutting for green concrete. We utilize advanced cutting techniques and precision equipment to create clean and accurate cuts, preventing future cracking and minimizing the risk of costly repairs.
Our experienced technicians understand the importance of timely intervention and will promptly schedule your expansion joint cutting project. We are committed to providing reliable and efficient services, ensuring the protection of your concrete structure.

Custom Projects

Asphalt Cutting

Our fully mobile asphalt cutting services provide exceptional versatility and convenience. Equipped with portable water supply, we can efficiently cut asphalt anywhere, anytime, regardless of location or accessibility.
We specialize in creating trenches of any size to accommodate civil services, parking lot repairs, or other infrastructure modifications. Our advanced cutting equipment allows us to cut through asphalt up to 15 inches thick, ensuring precise and efficient results.
With our expertise and commitment to quality, we deliver exceptional asphalt cutting solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Jackhammering

When faced with particularly dense concrete or confined spaces where traditional cutting methods are impractical, our hydraulic jackhammering services provide a powerful and effective solution. Our advanced jackhammering equipment is capable of breaking down concrete into manageable pieces, even in challenging conditions.
Our skilled technicians utilize specialized techniques and safety protocols to ensure precise and efficient jackhammering operations. We minimize noise and dust pollution while prioritizing the safety of both our workers and your property.

Concrete Removal and Disposal

To streamline your project and minimize hassle, we offer comprehensive concrete removal services as an additional option. Our team can arrange for a suitable bin to be delivered to your site, efficiently load the removed concrete, and coordinate its pickup.
We understand the importance of a clean and efficient worksite. Our dedicated team will handle all aspects of concrete removal, ensuring a hassle-free experience for you. With our complete removal services, you can focus on other project aspects while we take care of the concrete disposal.

Driveway Removal

Is your driveway marred by unsightly cracks and unevenness? Our driveway repair services offer a comprehensive solution to restore your driveway’s appearance and functionality.
We specialize in cutting or breaking up cracked or damaged driveway surfaces, allowing for effective repairs and resurfacing. Our skilled technicians utilize advanced equipment and techniques to ensure precise and efficient removal of the damaged concrete.
Once the damaged concrete is removed, we can assist you in coordinating the re-pouring process, ensuring a smooth and seamless transition to a renewed driveway. Our team is committed to delivering high-quality results that enhance both the aesthetic appeal and durability of your driveway.
