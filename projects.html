Projects

Aldergrove Town Centre - Parkade Modification

Scope of work included the following:

  • Removal of a large square section of the parkade concrete slab.
  • Cutting and removal of a wall section that extended beyond the required dimensions.
  • Modification of the double door frame to accommodate a taller door height.
  • Core drilling of 1 1/4" diameter holes through the slab for anchor bolt installation.
  • Notching of the footing to allow for removal of the incorrectly poured section.

Metal Track Replacement Project

In collaboration with Roaron Construction, City Concrete Cutting successfully replaced a metal track that was embedded in the concrete substrate. To meet the engineer's specifications, the track was installed at an angle. To ensure precise cutting and installation, a custom guide board was fabricated to assist the saw operator.
This project demonstrated our ability to execute complex tasks in partnership with industry professionals, while adhering to stringent engineering requirements.
